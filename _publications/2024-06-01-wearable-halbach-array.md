This paper presents a wearable electromagnetic Halbach array structure designed to wirelessly charge implantable biomedical devices. The proposed design improves the magnetic field distribution around the body, enabling efficient power transfer to implanted receivers.

**Keywords**: Wireless Power Transfer; Electromagnetic Halbach Array; implantable biomedical devices; wearable
